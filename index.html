The Goal

We want to find a standard list (for example, a list of traits such as Allport and Cattell) OR ontology to describe "behavioral phenomena" to add to the Cognitive Atlas.

My Opinion Thus Far

I am leaning toward existing ontologies because a list of traits may not capture behavioral experiences like preferences or sleep, and further, I can't pin down the ~4500 traits originally filtered by Allport.

This Interface
